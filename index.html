ABOUT DEPARTMENT

The Department of Biochemistry, University of Kerala has a distinguished tradition lasting over three decades of research and teaching on various aspects of Biochemistry. Right from the beginning, the Department has been concentrating itself on research problems of human relevance. The major areas of research include biochemical aspects of lifestyle diseases like atheroschlerosis, diabetes, arthritis and cancer, cataract etc.
